The movie, Matilda is about a telekinetically gifted little girl whose parents cared very little for her because they were too simple minded and negligent. She goes to school and makes friends forming a bond with her class teacher and earning the antagonism of her Principal. She eventually uses her gift to put an end to the Principal's tyrannical rule.
